Abstract

The publicly available datasets related to hyperspectral images have been organized and compiled in a list format. To help users select datasets based on specific research objectives or application tasks, the following information has been summarized in a table in Markdown format: "Name", "Year", "Task", and "URL." However, datasets related to aerial or satellite imagery are not included. Additionally, references to the sources of each dataset are provided, allowing easy access to relevant papers or distribution pages for datasets of interest.
This list is intended to support researchers at the initial stages of working with hyperspectral images.
